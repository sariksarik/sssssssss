Awaiting big news from the Treasury Department…

It's expected that the Treasury Department will clarify what a 'cryptocurrency broker' is sometime in January. Due to the passage of the infrastructure bill, these 'brokers' will be required to provide tax reporting information to customers and the IRS starting in 2024. We will be commenting on this news as it comes out. To stay up-to-date with the latest Treasury Department and IRS guidance, follow us on Twitter.
